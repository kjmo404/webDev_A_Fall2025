It can feel real as anything else. But what we're doing is building these elaborate grotesque fantasies out of our own unhappiness. - Dale Beran

This quote stood out to me because it highlights how our relationships with technology can become distorted over time. Platforms like 4chan were originally created as spaces for connection through sharing memes, but as the user base expanded and different groups began shaping the culture, the site became increasingly racialized and toxic. What began as a community built around creativity and fun eventually drifted far from its purpose. Instead of bringing people together, it ended up dividing them and amplifying harmful behavior.
